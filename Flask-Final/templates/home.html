Project Description

Credit Card Usage Trends in Indian Cities: The dataset provides a detailed look into credit card transactions in various cities of India, showcasing interesting trends and patterns. Delhi emerges as a key player in credit card usage, with substantial transactions across different card types, particularly in gold, silver, and platinum categories. Mumbai also shows significant activity, especially in platinum and gold card transactions. Bengaluru, while demonstrating lower overall transaction volumes compared to Delhi and Mumbai, still exhibits a notable preference for silver and gold cards. The dataset also indicates a higher usage of credit cards among females, suggesting a trend towards women being more active users of credit cards. The time analysis reveals consistent credit card usage over the dataset's duration from April 2014 to May 2015. This storyline paints a picture of diverse credit card usage patterns across different cities in India, highlighting the varying preferences and trends in card types and usage behaviors.
